This is a living document where I collect mathematics resources essential for data science and analytics.

## Linear Algebra

- [3Blue1Brown Linear Algebra Series](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLZHQObOWTQDPD3MizzM2xVFitgF8hE_ab) - Excellent visual introduction to linear algebra
- [Gilbert Strang Linear Algebra Lectures](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/18-06-linear-algebra-spring-2010/) - MIT OpenCourseWare lectures
- [Khan Academy Linear Algebra](https://www.khanacademy.org/math/linear-algebra) - Free online course

## Calculus

- [3Blue1Brown Essence of Calculus](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLZHQObOWTQDMsr9K-rjG-dK-7cliig9ef) - Visual intuition for calculus concepts
- [Khan Academy Calculus](https://www.khanacademy.org/math/calculus-1) - Comprehensive calculus courses

## Statistics and Probability

- [StatQuest with Josh Starmer](https://www.youtube.com/c/StatQuestwithJoshStarmer) - Intuitive explanations of statistical concepts
- [Seeing Theory](https://seeing-theory.brown.edu/) - Interactive visualizations of probability and statistics

## Reference Books

- [Introduction to Linear Algebra](https://math.mit.edu/~gs/linearalgebra/) - Gilbert Strang's classic textbook
- [All of Statistics](https://www.springer.com/gp/book/9781441923226) - Concise statistical reference

## Practice Problems

- [Brilliant.org](https://brilliant.org/) - Interactive math and science problems
- [Paul's Online Math Notes](https://tutorial.math.lamar.edu/) - Comprehensive math notes and practice
